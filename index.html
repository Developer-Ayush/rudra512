॥ The Eight Paths ॥

Architecture (v5 Parity)

01
SSI

Scattered Salt

Salt bytes are dynamically placed at independent positions via a 4-word SipHash-derived scatter and quadratic-style stride probing algorithm.

pos[i] = f(data_hash, salt_hash, i)
02
TPL

Token Pipeline

Buffer splits into tokens. Each undergoes a Fisher-Yates byte shuffle and rolling SipHash-driven XOR before absorption.

shuffle(token, fisher_yates(th))
03
SHM

SipHash-2-4 Mixer

A true 4-word SipRound engine replaces generic mixing, guaranteeing excellent avalanche properties for internal metadata.

sip4_compress(v0, v1, v2, v3);
04
WHT

Scaling Whitening

All 8 words are XOR'd with specific constants and salt tweaks, then fully permuted for proportional clamp(2 + R/16, 2, R) rounds.

s[i] ^= WK[i] ^ rotl(salt_h, ...)
05
SYM

Two-Snapshot Permute

Permutations capture explicit round snapshots before each loop to ensure symmetric processing and absolute parity across words.

s[i] += rotl(mid[(i+mix3)%8],...)
06
HFA

HAIFA Finalization

A running entropy accumulator feeds into a rigorous double finalization process, neutralizing standard length-extension attacks.

s[0] ^= orig_len ^ DOM_FINAL
